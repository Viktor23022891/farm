Visitor Information

Farm Name is located in a picturesque area between Edinburgh and Carlisle, offering a charming escape from the hustle and bustle.

Opening Hours

  • Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Saturday - Sunday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Holidays: Special hours may apply. Please check our website or call us for details.

Admission Prices

  • Adults (18+): $15
  • Children (3-17): $10
  • Seniors (65+): $12
  • Children under 3: Free
  • Group Discounts: Groups of 10 or more receive a 10% discount. Contact us for group bookings.

How to Find Us

We are located at:

123 Country Lane, Greenfield, UK

Directions:

  • By Car: From downtown Greenfield, take Highway 45 and exit at Farm Road. Free parking is available on-site.
  • By Public Transport: Bus #12 stops near the entrance to our farm.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable clothes and shoes for outdoor activities.
  • A hat, sunscreen, and water bottle for warm days.
  • A camera to capture memories.
  • Smiles and excitement!

Farm Rules

  • Do not feed the animals unless food is provided by farm staff.
  • Stay on marked trails and paths.
  • Supervise children at all times.
  • Dispose of trash in designated bins.
  • Pets are not allowed, except for service animals.

Facilities

  • Parking: Free parking is available.
  • Restrooms: Clean and accessible restrooms are located near the entrance.
  • Cafe: Enjoy delicious snacks, meals, and beverages at our farm cafe.
  • Gift Shop: Take home a souvenir to remember your visit.

Special Events

We host seasonal and holiday events throughout the year! Check our calendar for upcoming events like pumpkin picking, Christmas markets, and more.
